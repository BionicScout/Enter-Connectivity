What do we do?

Enter-connectivity is a program that was developed to promote the continued strengthening of relationships. A user will be able to keep track of all the people they wish to interact with on a consistent basis. The program will update the user on birthdays, which people to contact, and how long it has been since the two individuals last interacted.

Steps to Use

Step 1 - Create an Account
Sign up for an account. All you have to do is create unique username and password.
Step 2 - Add Contacts
Go to the Contact Page and click on the add button. Here you can create a list of contacts you want to talk to more often. Simply record their contact information and how often you want to talk to them.
Step 3 - Get a List of Requested Contactst
Go to the Get Contacts Page. Here, you can click a button to get a list of people you should contact.
Step 4 - Connect with your Friends and Family
Now you can stay up to date with your friends and family. Make sure you come back here to update when you last contacted them.
